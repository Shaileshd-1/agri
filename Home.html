About Our College

Welcome to Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Agriculture College, a premier institution dedicated to cultivating knowledge, innovation, and future agricultural leaders. Named in honor of the great freedom fighter Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose, our college carries forward his legacy of courage, vision, and commitment to nation-building. Established with the mission to empower rural and urban youth with world-class education in agricultural sciences, we offer a comprehensive B.Sc. (Hons) Agriculture program. This four-year degree combines academic excellence with hands-on training, fieldwork, and research exposure to equip students for successful careers in agriculture, agribusiness, and allied sectors. Our curriculum is designed as per ICAR (Indian Council of Agricultural Research) guidelines, focusing on core areas such as Agronomy, Horticulture, Soil Science, Plant Pathology, Entomology, Agricultural Economics, and Extension Education.
